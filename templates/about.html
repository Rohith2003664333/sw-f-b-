About Swaraksha

Empowering Safety and Security

Swaraksha is a comprehensive emergency response application designed to enhance the safety and security of women and children. Our platform provides essential features such as real-time location tracking, immediate SOS alerts, and access to emergency services. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, Swaraksha aims to ensure that users can quickly reach out for help during critical situations. The app also includes features to identify nearby police stations, crime alerts, and a community support network, all working together to create a safer environment for everyone.
